Pattern Blocker

One pattern per line. Empty lines or lines starting with # are ignored.
You can use:

  • Domain+path: youtube.com/shorts/ (blocks only Shorts)
  • Adblock-like anchors: ||tiktok.com/* (blocks entire domain)
  • Regex: /^https?:\\/\\/([^\\/]+\\.)?facebook\\.com\\/reel\\//

Tip: Click the toolbar icon to toggle blocking. “OFF” badge means disabled.
